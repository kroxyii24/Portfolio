01 — Who is Kroxyii

THE PERSON
BEHIND THE LENS

"I don't just make content — I create experiences that actually feel real."

Hey, I'm Eklavya — but online the world knows me as Kroxyii. I'm a creative based in Jaipur with a deep obsession for making things look sharp, feel intentional, and hit harder than expected.

My world splits between cameras, kitchens, open highways, event floors, and fitting rooms. Whether I'm plating a dish, shooting a ride through Rajasthan, directing a live production for thousands, or putting together a fit — the goal is always the same: make it memorable.

I've led large-scale events, won design competitions, and built a creative identity that refuses to fit in a single box. That's kind of the whole point.
